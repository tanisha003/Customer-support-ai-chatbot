# Returns & Refunds Policy

## Return Window

You can return most items within **15 days** of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Some categories have different windows:

- **Electronics & Appliances:** 10 days
- **Apparel & Footwear:** 30 days
- **Books & Stationery:** 7 days
- **Personal care, intimates, perishables, and customised items:** non-returnable

The return window starts on the day the order is delivered, as recorded by the courier.

## Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the item must be:
- Unused and in its original condition
- In the original packaging with all tags, manuals, accessories, and free gifts
- Accompanied by the original invoice or order ID

Items that show signs of use, are damaged by the customer, or are missing accessories will be rejected on inspection and returned to the customer at the customer's expense.

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your account and go to **My Orders**
2. Select the order and click **Return** next to the relevant item
3. Choose a reason and upload photos if requested (mandatory for damaged items)
4. Select pickup or self-ship — pickup is free in most PIN codes
5. Pack the item securely and hand it to our courier when they arrive

You will receive an SMS and email confirming the pickup, followed by another notification when the item reaches our warehouse.

## Refund Timelines

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 2 business days. The actual credit timeline depends on your payment method:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| UPI | 1–2 business days |
| Credit/Debit Card | 5–7 business days |
| Net Banking | 3–5 business days |
| Wallet | 1 business day |
| Cash on Delivery | 5–7 business days (refunded to bank account, details collected during return) |

## Exchange

Exchanges are available only for **size or colour changes** in the same product, subject to availability. To exchange, follow the return flow and select **Exchange** instead of **Refund** when prompted. The replacement item is shipped only after we receive the original item.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If the item arrives damaged, defective, or significantly different from the description, you are entitled to a free replacement or full refund regardless of the standard return window. Please report within 48 hours of delivery with photos and a brief description.

## Items Received in Error

If you received the wrong item, please report it within 7 days. We will arrange free pickup of the wrong item and ship the correct one at no additional cost.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following are not eligible for return under any circumstances unless damaged or defective:
- Innerwear, lingerie, swimwear, and socks
- Cosmetics, skincare, and fragrances once opened
- Food, beverages, and grocery items
- Customised, personalised, or made-to-order products
- Digital products, gift cards, and downloadable software
- Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page

## Refund of Shipping Charges

Original shipping charges are refunded only if the return is due to our error (wrong item, damaged item, or quality issue). For all other returns, the original shipping charge (if any) is retained.

## Need Help?

For any return-related queries, contact our support team at returns@example.com or via the in-app chat. Support hours: 7 AM to 11 PM IST, all days.
