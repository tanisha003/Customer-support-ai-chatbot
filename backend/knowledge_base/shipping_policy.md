# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Coverage Area

We currently ship to all serviceable PIN codes within India. International shipping is available to 22 countries including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and Singapore.

## Domestic Shipping Timelines

| Order Value | Standard Delivery | Express Delivery |
|---|---|---|
| Below ₹499 | ₹49 shipping, 4–6 business days | ₹149, 1–2 business days |
| ₹499 – ₹1,999 | Free, 3–5 business days | ₹99, 1–2 business days |
| Above ₹1,999 | Free, 2–4 business days | Free, 1–2 business days |

Metro cities (Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Pune, Hyderabad, Kolkata) typically receive deliveries 1 day faster than the timelines above.

## International Shipping

International orders are shipped via DHL or FedEx Express. Standard delivery takes 7–14 business days depending on the destination country. Customs duties, if applicable, are payable by the recipient at the time of delivery.

## Order Processing

All orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation, except on Sundays and national holidays. Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email.

## Tracking Your Order

You can track your order anytime by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Tracking links are also available in your shipment confirmation email.

## Failed Deliveries

Our courier partners attempt delivery up to three times before returning the package to our warehouse. If you are unable to receive the package, please contact support within 48 hours to schedule a re-delivery (subject to additional charges) or initiate a refund.

## Delayed Shipments

If your order has not arrived within 2 business days after the promised delivery date, please contact support@example.com with your order number. We will investigate with the courier and provide a resolution within 24 hours.

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD is available on orders up to ₹10,000 for serviceable PIN codes within India. A nominal handling fee of ₹40 applies to all COD orders. COD is currently not available on international shipments or on orders containing electronics above ₹5,000.

## Damaged or Missing Items

If you receive a damaged package or notice missing items, please report it within 48 hours of delivery via the "Report an Issue" option in your order details. Include clear photos of the package and contents. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund based on item availability.
