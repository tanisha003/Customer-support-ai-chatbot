# Product FAQ — PrimeStay

## General

**Q: What is PrimeStay?**
PrimeStay is an online property rental marketplace connecting renters with verified property owners across India. We list apartments, villas, PG accommodations, and short-term vacation rentals.

**Q: Is PrimeStay free to use for renters?**
Yes, browsing and booking through PrimeStay is completely free for renters. We charge property owners a small service fee on confirmed bookings.

**Q: In which cities is PrimeStay available?**
PrimeStay is currently live in 14 cities: Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Pune, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Kochi, Goa, Chandigarh, Lucknow, and Indore. We are expanding to 6 more cities by Q3 2026.

## Account & Booking

**Q: Do I need an account to book a property?**
Yes, you need a verified PrimeStay account to confirm any booking. Sign-up is free and takes under 2 minutes using email or phone OTP.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
Account verification requires a valid government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Driving License) and a selfie. Verification is usually completed within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I cancel a booking?**
Yes, free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before check-in for most listings. Some properties have stricter cancellation policies which are clearly displayed on the listing page before booking.

**Q: How do I contact a property owner?**
Once your account is verified, you can chat with the owner directly through the in-app messenger. Phone numbers are shared only after a booking is confirmed.

## Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
We accept UPI, all major credit and debit cards, net banking, and select wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay). EMI options are available on bookings above ₹15,000.

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways (Razorpay and Stripe). PrimeStay never stores your card details on its servers.

**Q: When is the payment charged?**
For monthly rentals, the first month's rent and security deposit are charged at booking confirmation. For short-term stays, the full amount is charged at booking.

## Listings

**Q: How do I list my property on PrimeStay?**
Sign up as a property owner, complete KYC, then use the "Add Listing" flow to upload photos, set pricing, and publish. Listings go live within 24 hours after our team's quality review.

**Q: Are listings verified?**
Yes, every listing is verified by our local team through a physical visit and document check before going live. Verified listings carry a green "PrimeVerified" badge.

**Q: Can I edit my listing after publishing?**
Yes, you can edit photos, description, and availability anytime from the owner dashboard. Price changes take effect for new bookings only.

## Support

**Q: How can I reach customer support?**
Customer support is available 7 AM to 11 PM IST, seven days a week. You can reach us via in-app chat, email at support@primestay.example, or phone at 1800-XXX-XXXX (toll-free).

**Q: What if I have a dispute with a property owner?**
PrimeStay offers a Resolution Center for any disputes. File a complaint within 7 days of check-in and our team will mediate within 72 hours. In serious cases, we offer a partial or full refund through our Renter Protection program.
